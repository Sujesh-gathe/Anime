Monkey .D Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, also known as " Straw Hat Luffy" and commonly as "Straw Hat", is the main protagonist of the manga and anime, One Piece. He is the founder and captain of the increasingly infamous and powerful Straw Hat Pirates, as well as one of its top fighters. His lifelong dream is to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure left behind by the late Gol D. Roger.He believes that being the Pirate King means having the most freedom in the world.

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro, also known as "Pirate Hunter" Zoro, is the combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates, one of their two swordsmen and one of the Nine Senior Officers of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet.As a master of Three Sword Style, a swordsmanship style which he created during his childhood training in Shimotsuki Village, Zoro is among the three most powerful combatants of the Straw Hats, alongside Luffy and Sanji. He first gain a bounty of 60,000,000 berries after the Incident at Alabasta, it later increased to 120,000,000 berries after the Enies Lobby Incident and then 320,000,000 berries after the Dressrosa Arc.

God Usopp

"God" Usopp is the sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of the Nine Senior Officers of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet.He is the fourth member of the crew and the third to join, doing so at the end of the Syrup Village Arc. Although he left the crew during the Water 7 Arc, he rejoined at the end of the Post-Enies Lobby Arc.Usopp is the son of Yasopp and Banchina. He was born and raised in Syrup Village.He first gain a bounty of 30,000,000 berries under his Sogeking Persona after the incident at Enies Lobby. After the Dressrosa Arc, Usopp gains a bounty of 200,000,000berries under his own name. Currently, following the events from the Raid on Onigashima, his bounty is raised to 500,000,000 berries.

Black Leg Sanji

"Black Leg" Sanji, born as Vinsmoke Sanji, is the cook of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of the Nine Senior Officers of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet. He is the fifth member of the crew and the fourth to join. His dream is to find the rumored chef's paradise, All Blue, which is where East Blue, West Blue, North Blue, and South Blue meet, along with their wildlife. He is one of the top three fighters of the Straw Hats, alongside Luffy and Zoro. He first gained a bounty of 15,000,000 berries for his role in the Enies Lobby Arc, it later increased to 177,000,000 berries after the Dressrosa Incident. But the condition of his bounty was changed to Only Alive due to his biological father's interference. It was later reverted back to Dead or Alive after escaping from Big Mom's territory with a value of 330,000,000 berries. His current bounty is 1,032,000,000 berries after the events of the Raid on Onigashima
